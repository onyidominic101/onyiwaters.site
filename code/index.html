Popular Water Brands

Aquafina

Aquafina is a brand of purified bottled water products produced by PepsiCo. It was first distributed in Wichita, Kansas in 1994.

Arrowhead

Arrowhead Water is a brand of drinking water that is sold in the western United States. It is owned by The Mountain Valley Spring Company.

Dasani

Dasani is a brand of bottled water created by the Coca-Cola company. It launched in 1999 and is one of the most popular bottled water brands in the world.

Essentia

Essentia Water is an ionized alkalized bottled water brand sold by Essentia Water, Inc. It was founded in 1998 and headquartered in Bothell, Washington.

JUST Water

JUST Water is a eco-friendly bottled water company with a focus on sustainable packaging and ethical business practices. It was founded in 2015 by Jaden Smith.
